# A1 Tantra - Sacred Tantra for the Modern Seeker

## What is A1 Tantra?

A1 Tantra is a modern web platform dedicated to teaching authentic Tantra practices. It offers comprehensive guidance on four major Tantra traditions:

- **Kriya Tantra** - Ritual-based practices
- **Carya Tantra** - Service-based path
- **Yoga Tantra** - Meditation-focused approach
- **Anuttarayoga Tantra** - Non-dual teachings

The platform provides personalized sessions with detailed exploration of each tradition, helping modern seekers integrate ancient Tantra wisdom into their lives.
